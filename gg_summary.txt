Lorelai Gilmore (Lauren Graham) is a 30-something single mom who runs an inn with her best friend Sookie. Her daughter Rory (Alexis Bledel) is a teenager who is often mistaken for Lorelai's sister due to their close relationship.

The show follows the daily lives of Lorelai and Rory as they deal with the ups and downs of life, including Lorelai's relationship with her parents, Rory's high school experience, and their romantic entanglements.

The show is set in the small town of Stars Hollow, Connecticut, where everyone knows each other. Other notable characters include Luke, who owns the diner, and Miss Patty, who teaches dance.
