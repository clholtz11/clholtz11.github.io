“Once Upon a Time” weaves a captivating tale of a new world where fairy-tale legends intertwine seamlessly with modern life. Emma Swan (Jennifer Morrison), a bail bonds collector, finds her life taking an unexpected turn when Henry, the child she gave up a decade ago, unexpectedly appears. Henry is certain that Emma is the daughter of Snow White and Prince Charming, who orchestrated her escape to protect her from the Evil Queen’s spell. This spell had frozen the fairytale world in time, transporting it to the present-day town of Storybrooke, Maine. After taking Henry home, Emma decides to stay in town to keep a watchful eye on him. As she delves deeper into Henry’s story, she begins to uncover evidence that suggests he might not be entirely mistaken.
